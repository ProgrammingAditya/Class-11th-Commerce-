The Origin Story of Money Making

Bhai, insaan subah se sham tak kuch na kuch karta rehta hai. In saari harkaton ko hum kehte hain Human Activities. Par har kaam paise ke liye nahi hota — isliye isse do parts mein baanta gaya hai.

Topic 1 — Human Activities: Do Type ke Log

💰
Economic Activities

Wo kaam jo "Rokda" (Money) kamane ke liye kiya jaye. Jaise: Office jana, dukaan chalana, ya doctor ka patient dekhna.

❤️
Non-Economic Activities

Wo kaam jo pyaar, sympathy ya dharam ke liye kiya jaye. Jaise: Mummy ka khana banana (pyaar), ya kisi ki madad karna.

Topic 2 — Business Kya Hai?

"Business ek aisi economic activity hai jahan hum rozana (regular basis) par saaman banate hain ya bechte hain taaki Profit kama sakein."

Business ke 7 Asli Lakshan (Characteristics):
1
Economic Activity

Paisa hi sab kuch hai bhai.

2
Production/Procurement

Ya toh khud banao, ya kahin se saste mein laao.

3
Sale or Exchange

Apne liye banana business nahi hai — bechna zaroori hai.

4
Regularity

Ek baar purana phone bech diya toh tum businessman nahi ban gaye. Roz ka kaam hona chahiye.

5
Profit Motive

Bina munafe ke business "Charity" ban jaata hai.

6
Uncertainty of Return

Zaroori nahi har baar paisa barse — kabhi kabhi dhanda manda bhi hota hai.

7
Element of Risk

Risk hai toh ishq hai! Business mein loss ka dar hamesha rehta hai.

Topic 3 — Business vs Profession vs Employment

🏪
Business

Dukaan kholi, khud ke boss bane. No minimum qualification (Anpadh bhi hit ho sakta hai!). Reward = Profit.

👨‍⚕️
Profession

Doctor, Lawyer, CA. Degree chahiye aur "Code of Conduct" follow karna padta hai. Reward = Fees.

👔
Employment

Kisi ke neeche naukri karna. Boss ki suno, salary lo. No risk. Reward = Salary/Wages.

Topic 4 — Classification of Business Activities

Business ko do badi branches mein baanta gaya hai: Industry aur Commerce.

🅰️ Industry — Jahan Maal Banta Hai

1
Primary Industry (Natural)

Extractive: Dharti ya samundar se nikaalna — Mining, Fishing, Agriculture.
Genetic: Breeding karwana — Poultry farm, Dairy, Plant nursery.

2
Secondary Industry (Manufacturing)

Analytical: Ek cheez se kai cheezein nikaalna — Oil refinery mein crude oil se petrol, diesel.
Synthetical: Do-teen cheezein mila ke ek banana — Cement.
Processing: Steps mein kaam hona — Sugar industry, Paper.
Assembling: Parts jod ke product banana — Laptop, Car, TV.
Construction: Building, Dam aur Bridges banana.

3
Tertiary Industry (Service)

Ye industry ko support karti hai — Transport, Banking, Insurance. Inke bina Primary aur Secondary industry chalna mushkil hai.

🅱️ Commerce — Jahan Maal Bikta Hai

Commerce wo pul (bridge) hai jo Producer aur Consumer ko jodta hai.
🛒
Trade — Kharidna aur Bechna

Internal Trade: Desh ke andar — Wholesale aur Retail.
External Trade: Desh ke bahar.
→ Import: Bahar se mangwana.
→ Export: Bahar bhejna.
→ Entrepot: Bahar se mangwa kar kisi aur desh ko bechna.

🤝
Auxiliaries to Trade — Help karne wale Dost

Transport: Maal ko ek jagah se doosri jagah le jaana.
Banking: Paisa aur Credit provide karna.
Insurance: Risk se bachana — aag, chori ka darr.
Warehousing: Maal ko sambhaal ke rakhna (Godown).
Advertising: Logon ko batana ki "Bhai, hum ye bech rahe hain!"

Topic 6 — Business Risk (Darr Ke Aage Jeet Hai!)

Bhai, business aur risk ka wahi rishta hai jo chole aur bhature ka hai — alag nahi kar sakte!

Definition: Business Risk ka matlab hai wo possibility jahan humein "Inadequate Profit" (ummeed se kam munafa) ho ya fir "Loss" ho jaaye.

1
Speculative Risk

Isme dono chances hain — fayda bhi ho sakta hai, nuksan bhi. Jaise market mein naya trend aaya.

2
Pure Risk

Isme ya toh sirf nuksan hoga, ya fir kuch nahi hoga. Jaise aag lagna ya chori hona. Fayda hone ka toh sawal hi nahi hai yahan.

Topic 7 — Causes of Business Risk

🌊
Natural Causes

Ispe kisi ka zor nahi — Flood, Earthquake, Akal padna.

👤
Human Causes

Insaani galtiyan — Staff ne chori kar li, carelessness, ya strike ho gayi.

📉
Economic Causes

Market ka khel — Competition badh gaya, Fashion badal gaya, ya Prices gir gaye.

⚠️
Other Causes

Political pange, ya machine ka achanak kharab ho jaana.

📋 Question & Answer — Exam Ready

Q1
List any five characteristics of business.

(1) It is an economic activity. (2) Production or procurement of goods and services. (3) Sale or exchange of goods for value. (4) Dealing in goods/services on a regular basis. (5) Aim of earning profit.

Q2
Explain the meaning of 'Profit' in business.

Profit is the excess of total revenue over total expenses. It acts as a reward for risk-taking and a primary source of finance for growth and expansion of the business.

Q3
What is business risk? What are its causes?

Business risk refers to the possibility of inadequate profits or even losses due to uncertainties. Causes include: natural calamities, economic shifts (change in demand/price), human factors (theft, strikes), and other causes like political instability.

Q4
Distinguish between Primary and Secondary industries.

Primary Industry: Concerned with extraction of natural resources (Mining) and reproduction of living species (Poultry).
Secondary Industry: Concerned with processing materials already extracted to produce goods for final consumption (Cement, Steel).

Q5
Describe the various types of auxiliaries to trade.

(1) Transport — removes hindrance of place. (2) Banking — removes hindrance of finance. (3) Insurance — removes hindrance of risk. (4) Warehousing — removes hindrance of time. (5) Advertising — removes hindrance of information.

Q6
"Profit is not the sole objective of business, but it is a necessary one." Comment.

While earning profit is essential for survival, growth, and as a reward for risk, a business must also fulfill social and human objectives — providing quality goods, protecting the environment, and ensuring employee welfare. Without profit, a business cannot sustain; but without social responsibility, it cannot gain long-term reputation.

🏁 Chapter Summary — Ek Nazar Mein

Human Activities: Money-related (Economic) aur Love/Duty related (Non-economic).

Business: Regular dealing + Profit motive + Risk.

Industry: Primary (Natural) → Secondary (Manufacturing) → Tertiary (Service).

Commerce: Trade (Kharidna/Bechna) + Auxiliaries (Banking/Transport/Insurance).

Risk: Business ka part and parcel — manage karo, avoid mat karo.
