INDIAN ECONOMY ON THE
EVE OF INDEPENDENCE

(Flashback: 1947 se pehle ka scene)

Bhaiyo aur behno, ye chapter humein us dardnaak flashback mein le jaata hai jab Britishers ne India ko apne fayde ke liye use kiya. Unka ek hi agenda tha — Great Britain ko ameer banana aur India ko garib.

1. Angrezon Se Pehle Ka Swag

Low Level of Economic Development

British rule se pehle, India kisi ka mohtaj nahi tha.

Independent Economy: Hum apni marzi ke malik the.

Main Business: Kheti-badi (Agriculture) mein kaam tha, par hamare Handicrafts (haath se bani cheezein) poori duniya mein 'Brand' thi.

Muslin (Daccai Malmal): Bengal ka ye kapda itna 'Premium' tha ki ise sirf Royalty pehente the. Ise Malmal Khas bhi kaha jaata tha.

TWIST: Britishers ne aate hi economy ka structure badal diya. Hum ban gaye Raw Material ke Exporter aur unke ready-made maal ke Consumer.

2. Agriculture Sector — Kheti Ka Haal: Behaal 🌾

Angrezon ke waqt 85% log gaon mein rehte the aur kheti par dependent the — fir bhi kheti ki halat kharab thi. Kyun?
1
Zamindari System (The Real Villains)

Bengal Presidency mein saara munafa Zamindars kha jaate the. Unhe sirf 'Lagaan' se matlab tha — kisan mare ya jiye, unhe fark nahi padta tha. Fix date tak paise nahi diye toh zameen chheen li jaati thi.

2
Commercialisation of Agriculture

Angrezon ne force kiya ki Food Crops (Wheat, Rice) ki jagah Cash Crops (jaise Indigo/Neel) ugao — taaki unki England wali factories chalein. Isse khane ki kami ho gayi.

3
Technical Problem

Na koi irrigation (sinchai) thi, na fertilizer. Bas baarish ke bharose kheti chalti thi.

3. Industrial Sector — Hunnar Ki Maut 🏭

Angrezon ne India ko De-industrialise kar diya. Unke do motive the:
1
India = sirf Raw Material ka source

Sasta kaccha maal le jao India se, England mein banaao, mehnge mein becho.

2
Handicraft Ka Khatma

Hamara world-famous handicraft band ho gaya kyunki hum machine-made saste kapdon se compete nahi kar paye. Isse berozgari (unemployment) badh gayi.

3
Modern Industry — thodi bahut aayi

19th century ke baad Cotton aur Jute mills aayi. TISCO (Tata Iron and Steel Company) 1907 mein bani — ye important year hai!

4
Sabse Badi Kami

India mein Capital Goods Industry (wo factories jo machines banayein) bilkul nahi thi. Machines ke liye bhi hum unpar dependent the.

4. Foreign Trade — Loot-Paat Limited 🚢

India hamesha se bada trading nation raha hai, par Angrezon ne 'Restrictive Policies' laga di.
1
Monopoly Control

Aadhe se zyada trade sirf Britain ke saath hota tha. Baaki thoda China, Sri Lanka (Ceylon) aur Iran (Persia) ke saath.

2
Suez Canal ka Game (1869)

Suez Canal khulne se Britain aur India ka raasta chota ho gaya — aur unka control aur bhi tight ho gaya.

3
Drain of Wealth 💸

India se bahut zyada 'Export Surplus' hota tha — hum zyada maal bhejte the. Par us surplus ka paisa India par kharch nahi hota tha. Wo paisa Angrezon ke war expenses aur office kharche ke liye England bhej diya jaata tha.

5. Demographic Condition — Logon Ka Haal 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

1
First Census: 1881

Pehli baar officially logon ki ginti hui.

2
1921 — Year of Great Divide ⚡

Iske baad India ki population consistently badhne lagi. Ye date yaad rakhna!

3
Literacy: Sirf 16% se kam

Female literacy toh sirf 7% thi. Ekdum dukhad scene tha.

4
Infant Mortality Rate: 218 per 1000

Aaj ye sirf 33 hai — imagine karo us waqt ka haal.

5
Life Expectancy: Sirf 32 years

Aaj 69 years hai. Log bahut jaldi mar jaate the — bimariyan, kuch nahi tha.

6. Occupational Structure — Kaam-Dhanda 👷

Agriculture (70–75%): Sabse zyada log kheti mein the.

Manufacturing (10%) & Services (15–20%): Inka share bahut kam tha.

Regional Variation: Madras, Bombay aur Bengal jaise areas mein log kheti chhod kar industries ki taraf badhne lage — par Odisha, Rajasthan aur Punjab mein log kheti par hi tike rahe.

7. Infrastructure — Fayda Kiska? 🚂

Angrezon ne Roads, Railways, Ports aur Telegraph banaye — par unke fayde ke liye, India ke nahi.
1
Roads

Gaon tak nahi jaati thi — bas army movement aur raw material carry karne ke liye bani thi.

2
Railways (1850) — Sabse bada contribution

Pehli passenger train 16 April 1853 ko Bombay (Bori Bunder) se Thane tak chali. Isse log door-door tak jaane lage aur business bada — par isne Indian village economy ko khatam bhi kiya.

3
Post & Telegraph

Telegraph ka use sirf 'Law and Order' maintain karne ke liye hota tha — public ke liye nahi.

4
Inland Waterways: Flop Raha

Railways se compete nahi kar paya — jaise Coast Canal in Odisha — band kar diya gaya.

📋 Exercise — Question Bank

Q1
Britishers ka main focus kya tha?

Unka main focus tha England ka fayda. Impact ye hua ki hamari economy stagnant ho gayi aur kheti-badi ki halat kharab ho gayi.

Q2
National Income calculate karne wale bade naam?

Dadabhai Naoroji (Grand Old Man of India), William Digby, Findlay Shirras, V.K.R.V. Rao (inka estimation sabse best), aur R.C. Desai.

Q3
Kheti ruki-ruki si kyun thi? (Agricultural Stagnation)

Main villain: Zamindari System. Baaki — irrigation na hona, sirf Cash Crops (neel/indigo) par focus, aur koshish ki kami.

Q4
Independence ke time kaunsi modern industries chal rahi thin?

Cotton aur Jute mills, TISCO (Iron & Steel — 1907), aur thodi bahut sugar, cement aur paper industries.

Q5
Two-fold motive (Dugna fayda) kya tha?

1. India se sasta Raw Material le jao. 2. England ka bana-banaya maal India mein mehnge mein becho.

Q6
Handicrafts ruin ho gaye — agree karte ho?

100% Agree! Angrezon ne 'Discriminatory Tariff Policy' lagayi — Indian maal par tax zyada, unke par kam. Machine-made saste kapdon ne hamare hunaar ko market se bahar kar diya.

Q7
Infrastructure kyun banaya?

India ka bhala karne ke liye nahi — apni Army ko jaldi move karne aur ports tak raw material pahunchane ke liye.

Q8
Industrial Policy ki burai (Shortfalls)?

Heavy/Capital goods industries nahi thin, Public sector sirf Railways aur Ports tak limited tha, aur growth rate bohot slow thi.

Q9
Drain of Wealth kya hai?

India ka paisa bina kisi return ke England bhejna. Export se jo paisa aata tha wo unke war expenses aur salaries mein chala jaata tha.

Q10
"Year of Great Divide" kaunsa hai?

1921. Iske baad India ki population consistently badhne lagi.

Q11
Demographic Profile kaisi thi?

Literacy rate <16%, Female literacy 7%, IMR 218 per 1000, Life Expectancy sirf 32 years. Ekdum dukhad scene tha!

Q12
Occupational Structure kaisa tha?

70–75% log kheti mein the. Manufacturing aur Service sector mein bahut kam log the.

Q13
Independence ke time bade challenges kya the?

Low productivity in agriculture, lack of industries, poverty, aur kharab infrastructure.

Q14
Pehla official census kab hua?

1881 mein.

Q15
Trade ka volume aur direction kya tha?

India 'Net Exporter' tha raw material ka. Direction zyadatar Britain ki taraf thi — Suez Canal (1869) ki wajah se aur bhi easy ho gaya tha.

Q16
Kya Britishers ne kuch positive bhi kiya?

Haan — par galti se! Railways, Ports, Post & Telegraph, aur Administrative system (Rule of Law) hamare baad mein kaam aaya.

🏁 Conclusion — The Reality Check

Jab hum azad hue, hamari economy ekdum thaki hui thi. Kheti mein kisan garib tha, industries pichdi hui thi, aur roads-railways ka structure sirf unke matlab ka tha. Azadi mili — ab rebuild karna tha scratch se.
